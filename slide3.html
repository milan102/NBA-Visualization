Conclusions

The 1st highest scoring team, the Milwaukee Bucks, only had one top 30 highest scoring player

The 2nd highest scoring team, the Golden State Warriors, had three top 30 highest scoring players

The 8th highest scoring team, the Toronto Raptors, would eventually go on to win the championship

The 1st top 30 highest scoring player, James Harden, belonged to the 11th highest scoring team

The 24th top 30 highest scoring player, Mike Conley, belonged to the lowest scoring team

The 3rd top 30 highest scoring player, Giannis Antetokounmpo would eventually go on to win the MVP

Note that all data is from the 2018-2019 NBA regular season
